Your wrist becomes the antenna. Tilt for pitch, raise and lower for volume — the legendary instrument you play without ever touching it, now on your Apple Watch.

Play it with a wave of your hand.

  • ↔
    Tilt for pitch. Sweep smoothly across the range, or lock to musical scales — major, minor, and pentatonic.
  • ↕
    Raise and lower for volume. From a whisper to full voice, controlled by the height of your hand.
  • ◎
    Digital Crown & readouts. Set your ceiling volume, watch the pitch in Hz and note names as you play.
  • ⌚
    One tap from your wrist. Add a complication to your watch face and start playing instantly.
  • ✓
    Private by design. No logins, no ads, no data collection — nothing ever leaves your wrist.

Play it like the real thing

The theremin is played by moving your hands through the air near two antennas. ThereminAir maps that same gesture to your Apple Watch's motion sensors — so the motion of your hand, exactly like the original, becomes the music.
