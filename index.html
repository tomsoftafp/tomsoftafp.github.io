TomsoftAFP Support

Support for Apple Watch apps by TomsoftAFP. If something is not working as expected, send a note and include the app name, device model, watchOS version, and what you were trying to do.

Contact tomsoftafpsupport@gmail.com

Typical response time: 2 business days.

Setup Help

Make sure your iPhone and Apple Watch are signed in with the same Apple ID, Bluetooth is enabled, and both devices are running supported software versions.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart both iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Install the latest available version of the app.
  • Open the Watch app on iPhone and confirm the app is installed on Apple Watch.

Bug Reports

Please include the app name, the steps that led to the issue, and whether it happens every time. Screenshots are welcome when they help explain the problem.
